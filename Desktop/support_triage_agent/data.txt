HackerRank Tests:
Tests remain active indefinitely unless a start and end time are set. If expiration is set, candidates cannot access the test after the end time.

Adding Extra Time:
To add extra time for candidates:
Go to Tests → Candidates tab → Select candidate → Add Time Accommodation → Save.

Reinviting Candidates:
You can reinvite candidates from the Candidates section after modifying their test settings.

Test Variants:
Variants allow customizing a test for different roles. They reduce maintenance but require at least two variants to function.

Account Deletion:
To delete a HackerRank account created with Google login, first set a password using "Forgot Password", then go to settings and delete the account.

Interview Issues:
If interviews disconnect due to inactivity, session timeouts may be causing the issue. Adjust inactivity settings if available.

Platform Issues:
If the platform is not working or submissions fail, it may be due to a temporary outage or system issue.

User Management:
To remove a user or interviewer, go to account settings or team management section and remove the user.

Subscription:
Subscription-related requests (pause, billing) require contacting support.

Certificates:
If certificate details are incorrect, contact support for corrections.

Claude Privacy:
You can delete a conversation by opening it and selecting the delete option from the menu.

Data Usage:
Claude may use data to improve models depending on settings. Users can manage data preferences in account settings.

Workspace Access:
If access is removed from a workspace, only admins can restore access.

Bug Reporting:
Security vulnerabilities should be reported through official support channels.

Service Failure:
If Claude is not responding, it may be due to temporary service issues.

Visa Card Support:
If your Visa card is lost or stolen, report it immediately to your issuing bank or Visa support.

Fraud Handling:
Unauthorized transactions must be reported immediately for investigation.

Charge Disputes:
For incorrect charges, contact your bank or card issuer to initiate a dispute.

Traveller’s Cheques:
Report stolen cheques to the issuer immediately with details like serial number and purchase info.

Emergency Help:
Visa provides global assistance for lost cards, including blocking and replacement.

Minimum Spend:
Some merchants enforce minimum spend limits; this is set by the merchant, not Visa.

Out of Scope:
If a query is unrelated (e.g., movies, general knowledge), respond that it is outside the scope of the system.
